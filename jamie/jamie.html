11 Rules for Our Relationship

1. Neither partner should be afraid of bring up a topic. All feelings are met with empthy.

2. Compassion is always shown when the other person is hurt.

3. The relationship allows time for interests and friends without the other partner needing to be involved.

4. When a disagreement occurs, both sides show that they respect the other's opinions. Each side listens to the other and respects their feelings as valid.

5. Both partners consult each other when making major financial decisions.

6. Both partners work towards compromising and the relationship is not one sided (eg. one partner changes more than the other).

7. Neither partner swears or talks over the other.

8. Hanging up mid-conversation is considered immature and not tolerated.

9. Manipulation is not employed after a fight. Both partners consider their role in teh disagreement and blame is not placed on one person (unless it is clearly Max's fault!)

10. Both partners do not put each other down in front of other people

11. Lack of compassion, withholding 'I love you', or the silent treatment are never used as punishment after a disagreement.
